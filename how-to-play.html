Game Objective

In Chinese Food ‚Äì Chef Dudu, you take on the role of a cheerful chef preparing delicious Chinese dishes for hungry customers. Your mission is to follow each recipe step-by-step using fresh ingredients.

Your goal is to cook, assemble, and serve meals accurately and quickly. Complete each cooking task to progress through the levels and become a master of traditional Chinese cuisine.

Level Progression

The game features 25 exciting cooking levels across 5 different restaurant locations:

  • Street Food Stall (Levels 1-5)
  • Traditional Kitchen (Levels 6-10)
  • Modern Diner (Levels 11-15)
  • Festival Market (Levels 16-20)
  • Chef's Master Kitchen (Levels 21-25)

Special Abilities

  • Speed Chop: Quickly chop ingredients to prepare dishes faster.
  • Multi-Serve: Serve multiple customers at once for bonus points.
  • Ingredient Magnet: Automatically attract nearby ingredients for a short time.
  • Time Freeze: Temporarily pause the timer to manage orders without pressure.

Enemies

  • Food Critics: Watch out for harsh critics who can lower your restaurant rating if you serve slow.
  • Burnt Dishes: Avoid cooking mistakes that cause dishes to burn and upset customers.
  • Hungry Rats: Keep an eye out‚Äîthey can steal your ingredients if you're not quick.
  • Kitchen Fires: Be careful to prevent fires that can disrupt your cooking flow.

Achievements

  • Master Chef: Successfully prepare all dishes in a level without mistakes.
  • Speedy Cooker: Complete a level within the time limit with perfect orders.
  • Ingredient Collector: Gather all special ingredients hidden in the kitchen.
  • Customer Favorite: Serve every customer perfectly to earn a five-star rating.
